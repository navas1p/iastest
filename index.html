Serving Since 1979

The Sharjah Indian Association is the premier body representing the Indian expatriate community in the Emirate of Sharjah. Registered with the Ministry of Social Affairs, we are dedicated to the welfare, social, and cultural needs of Indians in the UAE.

From operating the largest Indian school in the region to providing critical welfare support, the SIA stands as a pillar of support and a bridge between cultures.

Our Mission and Vision

The mission of IAS is to work for the social, educational and economic welfare and advancement of Indian Nationals in the United Arab Emirates. Indian Association Sharjah has been dedicating itself to the service of the community members providing them all possible assistance within our reach. We have sought the help, assistance and support of the Consulate General of India, Dubai for providing other services which the Association cannot directly provide. The Association tries its best to improve the social life of the community members by conducting various programmes in which they can directly involve and contribute.

Educational facility for the children of the community members is ensured through our institutions, Sharjah Indian School and Gulf Rose Nursery. Quality Education is being providing at the lowest fee rates through our institution. All modern amenities of learning are introduced. The best available faculty, modern infrastructure, best transport facility at very reasonable rates, well equipped labs, well set libraries, best nursery tools for our kids etc. are features of our institutions.

Providing more educational facilities and expanding the level of education has been planned already. Association has already started to introduce University level education after plus 2 and the procedures are underway for starting an external centre of the University of Kerala under Indian Association Sharjah. As part of the infrastructure development, a new site for a new school is being finalized and the preparatory steps have been taken to start the same.

President's Message

Dear Esteemed Member, In the tapestry of our world, the threads of love and respect weave a fabric that can transform our existence into a truly beautiful and harmonious experience. The essence of a nation lies in the unity of its people, fostering happiness and confidence through mutual care. Within the vibrant tapestry of social and communal life, the Indian Association Sharjah stands as a beacon - a community that transcends geographical boundaries to create a home away from home. Here, unity flourishes through shared language, culture, tradition, and a collective appreciation that fosters respect for the individual rights and privileges of each member. Step into the Indian Association Sharjah, and you'll find more than a community; it's a sanctuary of friendship, a haven for shared sorrows, a refuge for assistance, and a source of solutions to life's challenges. It's a place where minds find peace and relaxation. What began as a simple gathering has evolved into an integral part of every community member's life, thanks to years of selfless service. Together, as a united force, the Indian Association Sharjah embodies the power of collective action. As a group, we can accomplish tasks deemed impossible for an individual and our association's history is a testament to the strength found in supporting one another. The core values of supporting, loving, and respecting each other, coupled with a commitment to serving the community, have become the guiding principles of our journey over the past 44 years. Simplicity beats at the core of our being, transparency forms the heart, modesty graces the face, and faithfulness courses through our veins. In studying the struggles of the less fortunate, we extend our hands to the most deserving, ensuring transparency and collaborative efforts in every endeavor. The Indian Association Sharjah doesn't just exist; it thrives. Our commitment extends beyond providing professional advice, guidance, and support. In partnership with the Consulate General of India, Dubai, we offer financial and other forms of assistance to those in need, to those facing financial hardships. Educational services are paramount, and the promotion of art, culture, and literature holds a place of priority. As the seasons change, I extend warm greetings to all of you and wish you the very best in all your endeavors. Here's to another year of unity, support, and shared success within our cherished Indian Association Sharjah. Warm Regards, NISSAR THALANGARA President
