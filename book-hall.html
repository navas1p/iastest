Booking Steps

Follow these steps so we can confirm your reservation quickly:

  • Choose the hall and fill in your event date, time, and expected attendees.
  • Describe your event needs (stage, seating style, AV, catering, parking).
  • Upload Emirates ID and Passport copies for verification.
  • Submit the request; our team will confirm availability and fees.
  • Receive confirmation, make payment, and collect the booking pass.
